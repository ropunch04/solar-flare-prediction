Progress Update 5 (02/02/2022)

I progressed quite far in my research since I've completed both the preprocessing and am in a comfortable position regarding my machine learning model. For preprocessing, I've successfully compiled a list of solar events (4 distinct classes - solar flare & cme, solar flare, cme, none) and matched them to active regions. Then, I fed this into my feed-forward neural network and implemented algorithms such as stratified k-fold cross validation. Through this, I've achieved an accuracy of about 97%. Moving forward, I will need to start researching more about the real-time implementation of my algorithm. Steps I must take include collecting HARP data in real-time as well as figuring out how to host my machine learing algorithm on a website. Additionally, I also hope to create a time-dependecy factor in my ML algorithm. Finally, I will then conduct relevant feature analysis to understand the scientific foundation of flare events.
