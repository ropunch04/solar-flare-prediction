Progress Update 4 (11/17/2021)

Since the SDO database had no bulk download option, I had to use web scraping to collect my images. I used HTTP requests the scrape the website's HTML code and collect the urls for every image. Then, without downloading the images yet, I sorted the images I wanted based on filtergram, resolution, and time (with a 1 hour cadence). Now, I've reduced my data to about 100 images per day. By simply changing the year, month, and date variables within the program I can compile all my needed data. The next step is to read the txt files from GOES containing flare event information (specifically, peak time and intensity). Then, I will attach a flare intensity label to every SDO image 24 hours before the flare. After this, my next big step will be extracting relevant features.
