Solar Phenomena Prediction

Overview

From providing the food chain with energy to keeping us warm, the Sun is responsible of all life on Earth. However, it is still a very energetic and explosive astronomical body. Due to its high temperatures, pressures, and internal behavior, events known as Solar Phenomena occur. These can include sunspots, solar flares, coronal mass ejections, solar wind, and more. Studying these highly energetic phenomena provides us with a greater understanding of why the Sun is able to support human life, and it also gives insight into how these pheneomena impact us on Earth.
