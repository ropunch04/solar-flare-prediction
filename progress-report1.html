Progress Update 1 (9/28/2021)

Last year, my research project was centered around predicting the stellar activity of both our Sun and distant stars ( astero.vercel.app). However, while we predicted the general stellar activity trend one may see in a stellar cycle, we never truly were able to predict specific phenomena. My new project aims at doing just that in order to complement and fully complete last year's project. By predicting stellar flares, I will have taken the prediction of stellar activity one step further, allowing for an even better understanding of our Sun and an early-detection method for incoming flares. Additionally, I plan to expand this into distant stars as well. My general procedure includes extracting features from Sun images, so in this research, I'll explore whether its possible to extract those same features from light curves themselves, allowing for distant star solar flare detection. Currently, I have this idea but I'm yet to find a data set and research into the true feasibility of the project.
